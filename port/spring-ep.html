Spring EP (Move 1, Move 2, Move 3)

fixed media, electronic extended play · year: 2020

Spring EP deals with spring as a musical motif. It is inspired by a strong earthquake that hit Zagreb on the second day of spring, 2020. It follows a maturation of the human psyche metaphorically explained through the transitional period of the year.

This EP has three movements. They depict early, mid, and late spring:

  • Move 1 focuses on destruction. Early spring often feels like it is still winter. The result is a harsher sonical structure.
  • Move 2 depicts mid spring. Nature is blossoming and in this fictional world, things destroyed by a catastrophe are being rebuilt.
  • Move 3 portrays the ever-growing warmth of spring, closing in to summer. The fictional world is rebuilt and the mind is healing.

Techniques

Everything was done using FL Studio. All the plugins used are stock. I gathered samples from the internet and recordings I have made at home. Material sampled included various foliage, field recordings, industrial sounds, as well as various random melodies, rhythms, and sound snippets.

For synths, I mostly used Morphine. Some of the effects I used include EQ filters, reverb, flanger, Love Philter, Gross Beat, and Hardcore.
