House chore for one

live electronics, structured improvisation · year: 2025

A drying rack is used as an instrument, played in various ways, and manipulated using pedals.

Media

Technical

The drying rack is used to dry wet cloth whose droplets fall on top of pots. The rack and pots are amplified using contact microphones. The signal is sent through a mixer where it is amplified and equalized. This signal is then sent through a chain of pedals:

  • Delay pedal: TC Electronic RPT-1 Nova Repeater
  • Dynamic processor: KORG mini KAOSS PAD
  • Distortion pedal: Boss Turbo Overdrive OD-2
