Resistance band for two

live electronics, improvisation · year: 2025

Two players are tasked to cooperate with each other; one plays the chair and the resistance band while the other samples and processes the signal.

Performed by: Yulian Prots, Emanuel Križić

Media

Technical

The raw sounds from the resistance band and chair being scratched and hit are captured using a contact microphone. The signal received is manipulated using a SuperCollider patch and controlled using a MIDI controller, the KORG nanoKontrol 2. The controller has different parameters assigned to it. It can record audio inputs into 8 buffers, which it can then play, mute, and solo. There are also control filters that can reverse playback, add a high pass and a low pass, and dictate the starting point of the sample.
