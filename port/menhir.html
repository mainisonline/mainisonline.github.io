Techniques

SuperCollider was used for sound creation. Ableton was used for the live mixing with a USB controller as the input.

Some techniques include microvariation with randomization using randomizing functions, polyphonic detuning achieved with additive synthesis, envelope shaping, amplitude and frequency modulation, and spatialization using Splay and Balance2 for stereo imaging. Patterns were used to assemble and further develop sounds. After the code was finished, the various sounds were recorded and placed into Ableton for volume mixing.
